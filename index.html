Prospective PhD Student — Computer Science & Engineering / Machine Learning

I am a Lecturer and researcher in CSE with a strong interest in developing theoretically grounded machine learning methods. My research focuses on transfer learning, domain adaptation, representation learning, and explainable AI, with an emphasis on designing models that are robust, interpretable, and capable of generalizing across diverse domains. I am particularly interested in bridging theoretical foundations with practical machine learning to advance trustworthy and data-efficient intelligent systems.

I received both my B.Sc. and M.Sc. in Software Engineering from the Institute of Information Technology, University of Dhaka, graduating with the highest academic distinction. My research has been published in a leading international journal, and I am actively pursuing new research directions in machine learning theory, transfer learning, and explanable AI. I am seeking Ph.D. opportunities to contribute to fundamental research while addressing challenging real-world problems through machine learning.
